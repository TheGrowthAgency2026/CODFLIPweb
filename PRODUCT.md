# CODFLIP — The Growth Agency

## Product Purpose
A Shopify app that automatically converts Cash on Delivery (COD) orders to prepaid using smart WhatsApp nudges with discount incentives. Built for Indian D2C brands to reduce RTO (Return to Origin) rates and recover margin lost to returns.

## Users
Indian Shopify merchants (D2C brands) running 100–10,000 orders/month. Results-driven operators who track margin, RTO rates, and fulfillment costs. Budget-conscious, want clear ROI proof before committing.

## Register
brand

## Brand Voice
Authoritative, precise, operationally-minded. Speaks to merchants as a serious financial tool — stable infrastructure, not a trendy growth hack. Every metric is specific (₹, %, numbers). Show business impact first; explain the mechanism second.

## Anti-References
- Consumer-social pill buttons and rounded-everything aesthetics
- Gradient-heavy "modern SaaS" designs
- Generic teal/purple dashboards with hero metric templates
- Startup-casual copy ("game-changing", "10x your growth")
- Decorative illustrations instead of product UI

## Tone
Direct, metric-driven, Indian-market-aware (₹ symbol, WhatsApp-first culture, COD as default). Professionally English. Confident without being aggressive.
